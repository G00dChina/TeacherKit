Make the sprites interactive

Bring your sprites to life by adding instructions (a.k.a. computer code) that tell the sprites what to do.

With Scratch, your sprites can dance, sing, jump, talk, fly, and much more.

Play with the blocks in the different categories. Or check out one of these ideas to make your sprite:
    Move
    Say something
    Make a sound
    Change color
    Change costume
