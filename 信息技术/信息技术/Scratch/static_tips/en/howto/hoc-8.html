Share your project

When you are ready to share your project with others, click the “Share” button at the top of the screen.

(If you haven't already, you'll need to create a Scratch account to share your project online.)

In this activity, you'll create an interactive holiday card that you can share with friends and family.

After your project is shared, you can go to the project page to add a title, instructions, and other notes.

Now you can send a link to your holiday card to family, friends, and others!

Finally, let's finish the Hour of Code by adding your project to the big collection of holiday cards.
