Add sprites

There are 4 ways to add a sprite to your card. To add a sprite, click on one of these buttons:

Here's what each button does:
Library:
       Choose a sprite from the sprite library
Paint:
       Draw your own sprite in the paint editor
Computer:
       Upload an image from your computer
Camera:
       Take a picture (if you have a webcam)
