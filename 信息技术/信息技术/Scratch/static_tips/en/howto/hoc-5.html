Add a background image

There are 4 ways to add a background image (a.k.a. "backdrop") to your card. To add a backdrop, click on one of these buttons:

Here's what each button does:

Library:
       Choose a backdrop from the library
Paint:
       Draw your own backdrop
Computer:
       Upload an image from your computer
Camera:
       Take a picture (if you have a webcam)
