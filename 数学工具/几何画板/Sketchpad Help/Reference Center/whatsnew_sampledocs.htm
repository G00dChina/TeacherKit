Sample Documents

Sketchpad comes with many sample documents that explore ideas drawn from a wide range of mathematics; demonstrate potential Sketchpad applications; and contain tips, techniques, and examples for using Sketchpad effectively. Familiarizing yourself with their contents by browsing through them at least once can provide you another valuable Sketchpad learning resource. To view the sample documents, choose Help | Sample Sketches & Tools.
