Picture Gallery

Pictures can be useful for mathematically diverse applications. This gallery—consisting of pictures for you to use as you explore mathematics—is divided into four sections, based upon suggested use, although you may find other uses as well.

You can drag and drop, or copy and paste, these pictures into your sketches. To copy an image, right-click (or Control-click) the image and choose Copy Image from the pop-up menu. Then switch to Sketchpad and choose Edit | Paste Picture.

Transformation

Pictures are ideally suited for the exploration of transformations. Use these pictures to create or to analyze designs involving translations, reflections, rotations, dilations, and self-similarity.

Perspective and Proportion

Some pictures involve interesting perspectives or proportions in natural and human phenomena. Use these pictures to investigate or create models of perspective drawings, proportions, similarity, and trigonometry.
