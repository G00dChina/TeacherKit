Transformation

Pictures are ideally suited for the exploration of transformations. Use these pictures to create or to analyze designs involving translations, reflections, rotations, dilations, and self-similarity.

You can drag and drop, or copy and paste, these pictures into your sketches. To copy an image, right-click (or Control-click) the image and choose Copy Image from the pop-up menu. Then switch to Sketchpad and choose Edit | Paste Picture.
