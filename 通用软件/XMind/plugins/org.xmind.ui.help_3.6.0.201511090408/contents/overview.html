Overview

Overview viewer is very good at navigating the map, esp. the big and complicated mind maps.Sometimes, it's also called Pan and Zoom viewer. Because it has two features.

  1. Pan, press the left mouse key, and move the mouse in the viewer, we can see the see the part in red box in the map editor.
  2. Zoom, if you scroll the mouse wheel when focuing in the overview viewer, you will find that the map will be zoomed in/out in the editor.
