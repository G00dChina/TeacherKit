Find and Replace

You can use the Find and Replace function in XMind to quickly update repeated information. Here's how:

  1. Open the map.
  2. Open the dialog with one of these methods:
    • Choose 'Edit > Find/Replace'.
    • Use shortcut 'Ctrl+F' (Command+F for Mac users).
  3. You can find and replace the text in topic, relationship, boundary, summary, and the notes.
